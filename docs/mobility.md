# Mobility (PhD/ECR Research Visits)

GAMEX supports short-term research visits among partner institutions to foster collaboration, skills transfer, and joint research.

Visits are intended to:

- develop joint research projects
- exchange methodological expertise
- strengthen long-term collaboration within the network
