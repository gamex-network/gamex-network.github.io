# GAMEX — Generative AI Modeling for Extreme Events

**GAMEX** is a Una Europa seed-funded initiative (2026) building an international research network at the interface of **Generative AI** and **Extreme Value Theory (EVT)**.

## Key activities (2026)
- **Scientific Workshop** — Bologna, 11–12 June 2026
- **Summer School** — Edinburgh, 8–11 September 2026
- **Online Seminar Series**
- **Short-term research visits** for PhD students and ECRs

GAMEX is designed as a platform for long-term collaboration, with a view toward future initiatives such as **MSCA Staff Exchange**, **Erasmus+**, and **Doctoral Networks**.
